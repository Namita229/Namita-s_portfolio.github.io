üì¶ Data Structures Used

Time Series Data Arrays

For OHLC (open, high, low, close) market data feeding into trading algorithms with efficient indexing.

Trie Trees

For NLP query routing and fast matching of user inputs to financial intents and commands.

Graph Structures

Portfolio Graphs: Nodes as assets, Edges as correlation weights. Used in diversification and cluster-based rebalancing.

Priority Queues

Max-Heaps for selecting top assets based on custom scoring (e.g., Sharpe ratio, trend strength).

Merkle Trees

For cryptographic verification of investment history, asset ownership, and transaction integrity.
